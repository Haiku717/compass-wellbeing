Who it’s for

When online therapy makes sense

Living rurally

Living rurally can be amazing, but it can be challenging to find professional mental health support nearby. Online therapy brings it to you.

Health challenges

A helpful alternative for those whose health conditions make travel, or busy and crowded venues, difficult to manage.

Comfort

Sometimes talking with a professional while staying somewhere you feel most relaxed and comfortable helps you to engage better.

What is online therapy?

The same support, wherever you are

Online therapy offers a convenient and effective way to access mental health support from the comfort of your own home. It provides confidential support for stress, anxiety and relationship issues through secure platforms, so you can focus on what matters without leaving your space.
