Meet your counsellor

Hi, I’m Alex

I am a father, a husband, a son, a brother and a counsellor. I am also an introvert, and I balance my work with people by spending free time camping, cooking, gaming and watching movies.

I am genuinely interested in hearing what interests you and which areas of life bring you satisfaction and happiness. My practice is person-centred, solution-focused and trauma-informed, which means your values and your perspective always come first.
